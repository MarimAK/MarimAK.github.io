Recent GIS Work


Transforming Underused Lots into Commuinty Gardens

A project under the supervision of the City of Mississuga to help find solutions for food shortage in Mississuga.

Safer Space

As a student associate for the Esri Canada GIS Centres of Excellence program, the goal of this project was to bring awareness to the risks indigenous communities face against the rise of climate change.

Predicting and Preventing Future Forest Fires

An independent project where I used QGIS, Landsat 8 and Sentinel satellite imagery, to research past wildfires that occurred in British Columbia in order to come to reasonable conclusions that can help predict future forest fires based on past wildfires patterns.

Experimental Pollinator Plot

A project that focused on providing Creative Hub1352 and The Toronto and Region Conservation Authority a research report that shows the different types of Pollinator Plot practices that can be implemented in Mississauga with respect to its changing climate and enviornment.
